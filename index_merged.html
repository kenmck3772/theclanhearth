Myths & Legends

Tales and lore from the Scottish Highlands

Ancient Lore Highland Tales

The Brahan Seer

Coinneach Odhar, the legendary Highland prophet who made predictions through a magical blue stone. His prophecies about railways, oil rigs, and the decline of clan power came true centuries later.

Associated Clan: MacKenzie

Period: 17th Century

Loch Ness Monster

Nessie, the mysterious creature of Loch Ness, has captured imaginations for centuries. First recorded sightings date back to ancient Pictish times, with modern encounters continuing today.

Location: Loch Ness, Highlands

First Recorded: 565 AD

About The Clan Hearth

Through Fire, We Are United

Ken McKenzie

Clan MacKenzie Descendant

Montrose, Scotland

The Clan Hearth is created and maintained by Ken McKenzie, an authentic descendant of Clan MacKenzie living in Montrose, Scotland. This platform combines traditional Highland heritage with modern technology to preserve and share Scottish clan culture with the world.

Our mission is to connect people with their Scottish roots, whether through bloodline or spiritual connection, and to celebrate the rich tapestry of Highland clan traditions that have shaped Scottish identity for over a thousand years.

"Troimh theine tha sinn aonaichte" - Through Fire, We Are United.
